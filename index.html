About Me

I'm a Senior Data Analyst based in Denton, Texas, where I help businesses turn complex data into actionable insights. I specialize in building seamless data solutions using Excel and Power BI, with a focus on integrating web and field forms to streamline compliance and regulatory processes. My background in financial analysis gives me a unique perspective on P&L management, budgeting, job costing, and automation, skills that help companies make smarter financial decisions.

When I'm not diving into datasets, you'll find me tracking every movie and show I watch on Letterboxd and Trakt (yes, I'm that organized about my entertainment). I love tinkering with home automation through Home Assistant and building small tech projects that solve real problems. My wife and I have been part of our church community since 2010, where I serve on the production team and we lead marriage groups together.
