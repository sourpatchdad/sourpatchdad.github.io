Home Assistant Smart Home

Comprehensive Home Automation & Financial Tracking System

Project Overview

A comprehensive Home Assistant installation managing every aspect of a smart home, from climate control and lighting to financial tracking and Tesla integration. The system features 7 custom dashboards, 50+ automations, and integrations with 20+ services including cryptocurrency tracking, NAS monitoring, security cameras, and advanced data visualization.

Main Control Center Dashboard

Family & Plant Tracking

Real-time location tracking for family members with custom avatars, plus plant watering schedules with visual indicators for 11 houseplants. The waterfall history card shows temperature trends across 5 rooms over the past week.

Plant Care Dashboard

Dedicated plant watering tracker with button-triggered timestamps. Each plant displays days since last watered with visual indicators. Features 11 different plants including Albus, Babies, Betty, Billie, Cobie, Luna, Miles, and Pearl.

Financial Dashboard

Budget Overview & Asset Allocation

Month progress tracking with custom bar-card showing spending percentage vs. month completion. Multi-level collapsible account structure using fold-entity-row cards for banks, investment accounts, credit cards, and savings. Features ApexCharts pie chart showing 12+ asset categories with real-time values.

Net Worth Flow (Sankey Diagram)

Custom Sankey diagram visualizing complete financial structure. Shows flow from Net Worth through Assets and Debt categories, breaking down into Liquid Assets, Non-Liquid Assets, Retirement accounts, College Savings, and Mortgage. Each stream is color-coded and sized proportionally to value.

Historical Account Growth

Mini-graph cards showing 65-week historical trends for all major accounts. Includes Assets, Net Worth, College Savings, Retirement accounts, 401(k)s, IRAs, Emergency Savings, Long-Term Savings, HSA, Bitcoin, and Robinhood. Each chart features custom gradient fills and color-coded growth indicators.

Grocery & Budget Dashboard

Category Budget Tracking

Three custom bar-card budget trackers for Entertainment ($400 budget), Groceries ($1,150 budget), and Shopping ($400 budget). Each bar uses color-coded severity levels (green/orange/red) based on spending thresholds. Integrated with Target Grocery List todo entity for shopping list management.

Tesla Dashboard

Battery, Climate & Tire Monitoring

Real-time Tesla integration showing battery percentage with custom bar-card, range and battery level mini-graphs with 7-day history, internal and external temperature tracking, and tire pressure monitoring for all four tires. Climate control integration allows remote temperature adjustment.

Sample Automations (50+ Total)

  • Smart Arrival: Automatically opens garage and turns on entry lights (Front Door Light + Entryway Lights) when family members arrive home
  • Midnight Routine: Turns off 17 different lights/switches at 2 AM, locks front door via Google Assistant voice command
  • Sunrise/Sunset: Automatically controls Front Door Light and Back Door Light based on sun position
  • Location Notifications: Sends iOS push notifications when family members arrive/leave work, school, gym, or church
  • Garage Safety: Auto-closes garage at 10 PM, 11 PM (Wed), midnight, and 2 AM if left open
  • Bias Lighting: Automatically turns on/off TV backlight when Living Room TV powers on/off
  • Tesla Climate Protection: Opens vent windows if interior temperature exceeds 110°F and exterior is above 95°F while away from vehicle
  • Financial Snapshots: Records midnight snapshots of Bitcoin price and portfolio values for historical tracking
  • Plant Care Logging: Timestamps plant watering events when buttons are pressed for 11 different plants
  • Pet Feeding Tracker: Logs automatic Wi-Fi pet feeder activations with timestamps
  • Room Light Groups: Button-triggered automation groups for Bedroom (3 lights), Kid Room (2 lamps), and Dining Room (2 lights)
  • Book Club Preparation: Opens garage door automatically at 7:55 PM every Wednesday for weekly book club

Implementation Highlights

  • Advanced Templating: Custom Jinja2 templates for dynamic card styling, conditional formatting, and calculated values
  • Real-time Data: Live updates from 20+ APIs including financial (CoinGecko, Yahoo Finance), weather, and IoT services
  • Historical Tracking: 365-day data retention with mini-graph cards showing trends up to 65 weeks
  • Complex Visualizations: Sankey diagram for financial flow, ApexCharts for asset allocation, waterfall history for temperature
  • User-Based Views: Conditional visibility based on logged-in user (financial dashboards restricted to specific users)
  • Custom Sensors: Template sensors for calculations like spending percentage, month progress, days since plant watered
  • Voice Control: Google Assistant SDK integration for hands-free door locking via voice commands
  • Notification System: iOS push notifications for location events, garage status, and arrivals/departures
  • Smart Device Integration: 18+ smart switches/lights, 4 cameras, 5 temperature sensors, 1 garage door controller
